Ingredients:
 - 1/2 teaspoon sugar
 - 1/3 cup milk
 - 1/2 cup water
 - 1 spoon tea leaves


Directions:
 1. Boil water in pot
 
 2. Add loose tea leaves - let steep for 1.5 minutes
 
 3. Add milk and bring to boil - when mixture starts to rise, turn down heat and let simmer for 2 minutes.

 4. User strainer and pour in cup. 

 Enjoy!
